More About Me

My name is Ryan Ramminger, and I'm currently pursuing my Master's in Robotics at the University of Birmingham in the United Kingdom. I am passionate about robotics, mechanical engineering, and machine learning/AI concepts. During my academic journey at the University of Birmingham, I actively engaged in the UBRobotics Club and showcased my commitment to teamwork and disipline by representing the University on its swim team.

I recently graduated from the University of Wisconsin-Green Bay (Spring 2022) where I received my Bachelor of Science in Mechanical Engineering along with minors in Math and Business. During my time at Green Bay, I was a part of the University's NCAA Division 1 swim team on campus and participated in many clubs such as Student Athletic Advisory Committee, Colleges Against Cancer, and Engineering Club.

Growing up, I worked with outdoor equipment ranging from cars and tractors to boats and dirt bikes. Much of what I have learned was aimed to solve problems presented to me. Learning from my mistakes and turning a negative situation into a positive one is the goal of any of my experiences. I take on every challenge presented to me with the same aptitude to create the best possible outcome.
