Frame by Frame

A Video Series Exploring the World of Computer Vision

Through short, accessible videos, I aim to simplify complex topics, share practical insights, and make the theory behind computer vision engaging and easy to understand for everyone. Whether you're new to tech or a seasoned pro, each video offers something to spark curiosity and expand knowledge in this exciting field.

Frame by Frame - Ep. 5: Advanced Edge Detection

-Computer Vision Series-

In this episode, we discuss advanced edge detection techniques, focusing on the Canny operator and the Laplacian of Gaussian. These tools allow for precise edge identification in complex images. As a bonus, we also explore separable filters, an optimization trick to boost computational speed.
